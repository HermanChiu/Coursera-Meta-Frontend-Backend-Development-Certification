Hair Day is a boutique hair salon that specializes in cut, color and styling. To keep clients inspired between appointments, the employees share makeup and nail tutorials on the Hair Day website. We are a staff of seven: two stylists, three colorists, one manicurist and one make-up artist.

A little about us

Hair Day also offers makeup and nail services, prides itself on its warm and relaxing atmosphere and is best known for edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts. The owner of the store, Pria, earned her certificate as a colorist and immediately started specializing in edgy, custom coloring techniques. The store was opened with Garry, her good friend and talented hairdresser.
